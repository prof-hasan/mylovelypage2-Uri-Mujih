1x1x1x1 is a purchasable killer, available for 1,250 Player Points Player Points. Specializing in long-range combat, he fires wall-piercing projectiles that deal heavy damage and can revive fallen survivors as hostile entities. His melee slashes are also effective, especially when combined with His other abilities. However, he requires some skills to master—particularly in predicting survivor movements to attack effectively.

In the Roblox community, 1x1x1x1 was long rumored to be a hacker following the account's creation on October 25, 2007.[1] However, the account was originally created by Shedletsky, Roblox's former Creative Director, as part of Roblox's early lore. In that lore, 1x1x1x1, also known as The Adversary, is an entity from the fourth dimension who seeks to destroy Roblox for existing in only three dimensions but was ultimately imprisoned by 2x2 at the dawn of time.[2]

Slash-M1

1x1x1x1 swings their sword infront of them, dealing 20 damage and inflicting Glitched I and Poisoned I for 5s each.

MASS INFECTION-Keybind Q-Cooldown 14s

Following a windup of 1.7s, 1x1x1x1 takes their two swords and performs a horizontal split with them, creating a sharp shockwave that has a lifetime of 6s and deals 35 damage per-slash to those it hits.

  • -Inflicts Glitched III and Poisoned V for 3s if 1x1x1x1 hits a player up close.
  • -Inflicts Glitched I and Poisoned I for 5s if 1x1x1x1 hits a player with the created shockwave.

ENTANGLEMENT-Keybind E-Cooldown 18s

Following a windup of 0.75s, 1x1x1x1 throws their two swords, which have a lifetime of 10s, forwards at a speed of 125 studs per second. If it hits a survivor, it deals 10 damage, and 1x1x1x1 gains Speed II for 3s.

The hit survivor has their aura revealed to the killer for 2s, gains Helpless I for 10s, and gains Slowness X. The survivor is required to do a 'minigame' to have their Slowness X removed.

he minigame requires the survivor to click on 5 pop ups on their screen to free themselves. After a survivor escapes ENTANGLEMENT, they gain Speed I for 1.25s.

UNSTABLE EYE-Keybind R-Cooldown 25s

1x1x1x1 digs into their glaring red eye and tears at it, revealing the auras of all living survivors to them and applies Speed I for 6.5s, while also applying Blindness III for 6.5s.

Parasitic

Every attack that 1x1x1x1 has inflicts Glitched and Poisoned to some degree.

REJUVENATE THE ROTTEN-Keybind T-Cooldown 200s

1x1x1x1 stabs themselves with their swords and pulls them out, resurrecting any players that they killed in the form of rotten zombies that serve them.

Summoned zombies wander aimlessly upon spawning and target players that are within a 30 stud distance from them. They lose focus on their target if the distance between them & the target exceeds a distance of 50 studs. Summoned zombies have 10 health and deal 14 damage to players.

Although the zombies are loyal to 1x1x1x1, 1x1x1x1 can kill them to gain Speed I for 10s, with the maximum stack being Speed III. The ability starts off with 0 charges and a charge is given on kill. Max of 1 charge.
