Noli

Noli in Forsaken is based on a Roblox myth of the same name, often regarded as one of the platform's earliest urban legends. The myth originated in February 2010, when a user named "YaleUniversity" made a post describing a mysterious account called Noli and the strange events surrounding it. The account was later deleted, presumably by Roblox. Since then, Noli has become one of the most well-known Roblox myths, with theories suggesting he is a hacker, a glitch, or a ghost.

In Forsaken, the true motives behind Noli's violent actions remain unclear. Recent developer input also implies that Noli sadistically views the games as a performance, with the survivors merely serving as extras in his show.[1]

Stab-M1

Noli uses his Shadow Tendril to stab forwards after a 0.4s windup, dealing 25 damage.

MASS INFECTION-Keybind Q-Cooldown 14s

Noli charges a rush for 1 second (can be cancelled).

  • Hitting a survivor deals 10 damage and applies Hallucination II for 20 seconds.
  • After 0.85 seconds, Noli can perform a re-rush.
  • Hitting a survivor with Hallucination II or higher deals 10 damage but also slams them into the ground, dealing 30 damage and ending the rush, dealing a total of 40 damage. (50 if the target got hit by both the 1st and 2nd dash.)
  • Rush ends early if Noli is stunned, crashes into a wall, misses hitting a survivor in time, or releases the rush key.
  • Turn rate is heavily increased for the first 0.8 seconds of the rush.
  • Noli can sprint during re-rush to temporarily boost speed.
  • Stamina does not regenerate during the rush.

ENTANGLEMENT-Keybind E-Cooldown 18s

Following a windup of 0.75s, 1x1x1x1 throws their two swords, which have a lifetime of 10s, forwards at a speed of 125 studs per second. If it hits a survivor, it deals 10 damage, and 1x1x1x1 gains Speed II for 3s.

The hit survivor has their aura revealed to the killer for 2s, gains Helpless I for 10s, and gains Slowness X. The survivor is required to do a 'minigame' to have their Slowness X removed.

he minigame requires the survivor to click on 5 pop ups on their screen to free themselves. After a survivor escapes ENTANGLEMENT, they gain Speed I for 1.25s.

UNSTABLE EYE-Keybind R-Cooldown 25s

1x1x1x1 digs into their glaring red eye and tears at it, revealing the auras of all living survivors to them and applies Speed I for 6.5s, while also applying Blindness III for 6.5s.

Halucinations

Many of Noli's abilities inflict Hallucination stacks on survivors.

  • Survivors with any Hallucination level reveal their aura to Noli. The highlight is darker for more intense levels of the effect.
  • Hallucination causes increasing hallucination effects as stacks grow.
  • Being hit by Noli removes all Hallucination stacks.
  • Hallucination caps at III.
  • At Hallucination I, the survivor's timer becomes unreadable.
  • At Hallucination II, fake healing objects such as a pizza or dispenser spawn to distract survivors.
  • At Hallucination III, a hostile mirage of Noli appears and attacks the survivor. Do not look at it! (Mirages don't deal damage, even if they appear so.) Even if there isn't a Dusekkar, survivors may also see a spawn protection shield around them. Looking at where the fake shield is coming from will be Noli's hallucination head, which will then fly towards the survivor if they continue looking at it, causing temporary blindness.

Prankster

At round start, Noli spawns Fake Generators in any place where all the 5 real generators could have spawned, but didn't.

Completing a Fake Generator puzzle grants Hallucination I.

Fully completing a Fake Generator instantly grants Hallucination III and resets the generators progress(after a short time).
